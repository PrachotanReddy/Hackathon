How to get started?

WINnovations is open to all skillsets and ideas: aspiring teams and individuals, corporate teams and students, ideas and projects. We are building a culture of innovation through collaboration.

1

Tell us who you are

Register on our portal. Don't worry, your privacy is of utmost concern to us!

2

Know what's troubling us

View the current statements on your dashboard, go through the detailed information and the requirements for the submission.

3

Get your hands dirty

Understand what we are looking for, get working on your submission. Make sure you meet the deadlines.

4

Sit back and get working on the next problem statement

Meanwhile, we'll go through your submission and get in touch with you.
